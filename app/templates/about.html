About Me

Who are you?

My name is Bryan Bugyi and I am a student of Computer Science.  I have been passionate about computers for as long as I can remember and consider myself extraordinarily fortunate to have the opportunity to make a career out of something that I love. 

Tell me more about yourself.

I am 25 years old and I live with my girlfriend Kelly.  She is my rock.  When I'm not studying or working on a project, my favorite things to do are to spend time with her and our Olde English Bulldog, Rocky.  After much deliberation, he has agreed to allow me to post photos of him on my website.  As you can tell from the picture to the left, he is quite overjoyed about the idea. 

When not with the family, you might find me at the beach surfing.  I have been surfing for 6 years now and have fallen in love with the sport.  There are few places I'd rather be than 50 yards out waiting for the next set to roll in. 

What else?  Well, I am a very passionate person.  I suppose you could say that I am the "go big or go home"  type with just about everything that I do.  On a related note, I have been told on more than one occasion that I can be a bit obsessive.  And I guess I would have to agree, but you know what?  Obsession can manifest as either a gift or a curse, or oftentimes both.  Moreover, I would even say that obsession is a necessary condition for the presence of passion.  Overall, I am incredibly grateful that both are traits that come so effortlessly for me. 

Tell me about this website.

This website is intended to serve as the central hub for my online identity, as an online host for my resume, and as a programming playground where I can test JavaScript code as I work to better understand the language and web development in general. 

This website is hosted on a cloud server that I am renting from DigitalOcean.  The content on this site has been served to you with the help of Flask, a lightweight Python web framework.
