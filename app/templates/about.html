About Me

Who are you?

My name is Bryan Bugyi and I am a student of Computer Science. 

Tell me more about yourself.

I am 25 years old and I live with my girlfriend Kelly.  She is my rock.  When I'm not studying or working on a project, my favorite things to do are to spend time with her and our Olde English Bulldog, Rocky.  After much deliberation, he has agreed to allow me to post photos of him on my website.  As you can tell from the photo shown to the left, he is overjoyed about the idea. 

When not with the family, you might find me at the beach surfing.  I have been surfing for 6 years now and have loved every single second of it.  There are few places I'd rather be than 50 yards out waiting for the next set to roll in. 

What else?  Well, I'm a very passionate person.  I suppose you could say that I am the "go big or go home"  type with just about everything that I do.  I have been told on more than one occasion that I can be a bit obsessive.  And I guess I would have to agree, but you know what?  Obsession can manifest as either a gift or a curse, or oftentimes both.  Overall, I am incredibly grateful that it is a trait that comes so effortlessly for me.
