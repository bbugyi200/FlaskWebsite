About Me

Who are you?

My name is Bryan Bugyi and I am a student of Computer Science.

Tell me more about yourself.

I am 25 years old and I live with my girlfriend Kelly. She is my rock. When I'm not studying or working on a project, my favorite things to do are to spend time with her and our Olde English Bulldog, Rocky. He's the one in the picture to the left, looking woefully unimpressed. That is his default status and we have learned to accept him for it.

When not with the family, you might find me at the beach surfing. I have been surfing for 6 years now and have loved every single second of it. I must admit, I'm still not that great and haven't had the chance to go out nearly as much as I would have liked over the last couple of years. Yet, even still, there are few places I'd rather be than 50 yards out waiting for the next set to roll in.

What else? Well, I like to get passionate about things. I am kind of the "go big or go home" type with everything that I do I suppose. For example, I don't just make coffee. I grind my locally roasted coffee beans to the perfect level of coarseness before brewing the best damn cup of coffee man hath ever laid eyes upon (at least I'd like to think so).

On that note, I have been told on more than one occasion that I can be a bit obsessive. And I guess I would have to agree, but you know what? Obsession can manifest as either a gift or a curse, or oftentimes both. Overall, I am incredibly grateful that it is a trait that comes so effortlessly for me. With that said, it would not be right of me to sugarcoat the ugly side of obsession. The truth is, finding the right balance between passion and delusion has been my lifelong struggle. But I can honestly say that it is one that I would not give up for the world.
